About Passions

Passions was started by a small-town bartender who imagined greater things. The company's name is what our business stands for. We are passionate about what we do. As a bartender, it is our job to ensure your event is special and to make sure your guests are having the time of their lives!

Why Choose Passions?

We will not only make and/or serve drinks for your guests. We will also provide you with advice on how much alcohol you may need, glasses and stemware you may need, as well as help set up your bar area to have everything placed conveniently and aesthetically for your guests. All bartenders are RAMP certified, come prepared, and are in attire to suit your theme/color scheme if you desire.
